Available Positions

At Breifne Bakery we are always seeking new talent & fresh ideas.

See the available listings below and apply if you believe you are ready to join our amazing team!

NPD Technician

Join our team as an NPD Technician and you will experience first-hand what goes into ensuring that every new product we put out is up to standard, and is one that we can personally stand behind. Your creativity is what allows us to continue to craft those truly special culinary experiences.

Maintenance Engineer

Due to the technical nature of the machinery used to craft our pizzas, we are always on the lookout for knowledgable maintenance engineers to ensure that our machinery is up and running. Furthermore, any improvements or modifications that are possible to be performed are always accepted with great enthusiasm, we need your help to identify these.

General Operative

As with any manufacturing plant, we cannot rely solely on the machinery to produce our products. We firmly believe that the human element is what truly creates a product worth buying.
