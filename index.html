"Baking with a passion since 1986"

Local Sourcing

We believe in the necessity of community, and as such are proud to source all locally-available high-quality ingredients from businesses in the surrounding areas.

Premium Imports

Some ingredients cannot be sourced locally, which is why we carefully decide which suppliers to obtain them from, as quality is foremost in our decision-making process. This results in only purchasing from the most reputable foreign suppliers to ensure the quality our products is second to none.

Eco-Powered

Safeguarding our environment is the key to our planet's future. We make sure to do our part and rely solely on renewable energy sources such as wind power locally sources to power all of our sites' machinery and infrastructure.

Vegan Capability

Everyone deserves to be able to purchase quality products. This is why we have a commitment to providing vegan-friendly products that even those who may not have been able to previously enjoy a slice of pizza may now do so with confidence.

Modern Technologies

Keeping up to date with the latest technologies may seem like a tough challenge At Breifne Bakery we always seek ways in which we can further optimise our work flow, allowing us to focus on what really matters; making high-quality products you love.

Large Production

Over the 30+ years we have been in the food manufacturing industry we have always strived to expand in a controlled manner. Our belief that everyone deserves to eat food which they love is what has always driven us to seek to provide this opportunity to as many as possible.
